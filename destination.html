Overview

Escape to the pristine paradise of the Maldives, where crystal-clear turquoise waters meet powder-white sandy beaches. This exclusive 5-day package takes you to a luxurious overwater villa in the South Malé Atoll, offering unparalleled privacy and breathtaking ocean views.

Experience world-class snorkeling in vibrant coral reefs, indulge in spa treatments with the sound of waves as your soundtrack, and dine under the stars on a private sandbank. Every moment is crafted to create memories that last a lifetime.

Whether you're seeking a romantic getaway, an adventurous dive trip, or simply a place to unwind and reconnect with nature, the Maldives delivers an experience unlike any other on Earth.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

1

Day 1 — Arrival

Welcome to Paradise

Arrive at Malé International Airport where our representative will greet you. Enjoy a scenic speedboat transfer to your overwater villa. Settle in and spend the afternoon exploring the resort or relaxing on your private deck. Evening welcome dinner at the overwater restaurant.

2

Day 2 — Adventure

Ocean Explorer

Morning guided snorkeling safari to discover vibrant coral reefs and swim alongside tropical fish, rays, and if lucky, gentle whale sharks. Afternoon at leisure — choose from diving, kayaking, or simply lounging by the infinity pool. Evening sunset dolphin cruise.

3

Day 3 — Relaxation

Wellness & Romance

Begin with a sunrise yoga session on the beach. Enjoy a complimentary 60-minute couples spa treatment. Afternoon free to explore the island or take a local fishing trip. Evening private sandbank dinner under the stars — a truly magical experience.

4

Day 4 — Exploration

Island Hopping

Full-day island hopping tour visiting local villages, a floating restaurant, and a stunning sandbank for picnic lunch. Experience the local culture, taste traditional Maldivian cuisine, and shop for handmade souvenirs. Return to resort for a farewell cocktail party.

5

Day 5 — Departure

Farewell Maldives

Enjoy a leisurely breakfast with ocean views. Last chance for a morning swim or spa treatment. Check out and transfer to the airport with unforgettable memories of your Maldivian paradise escape.
