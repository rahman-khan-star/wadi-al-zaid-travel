Our Story

A Decade of Turning Dreams Into Journeys

Founded in 2010 by Ahmed Al Zaid, Wadi Al Zaid Tours & Travel began as a small travel agency in Dubai with a big vision: to make world-class travel experiences accessible to everyone. What started with a team of 3 passionate travelers has grown into one of the most trusted names in the travel industry.

Over the years, we've helped thousands of travelers explore over 50 countries across 6 continents. Our commitment to exceptional service, attention to detail, and genuine love for travel has earned us a loyal customer base and numerous industry awards.
