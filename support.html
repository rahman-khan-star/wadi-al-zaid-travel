Frequently Asked Questions

You can modify or cancel your booking by going to "My Bookings" in your dashboard. Select the booking you want to change and click "Modify" or "Cancel". Please note that cancellation policies vary depending on the package and timing.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers, and cash on arrival for select destinations. All online payments are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.

Yes, we provide comprehensive visa assistance for most destinations. Our visa team will guide you through the application process, help you prepare documents, and track your application status. Visa fees are separate from tour packages.

Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days. The refund will be credited to the original payment method. For credit card payments, it may take an additional 3-5 business days to appear on your statement.

In case of flight delays or cancellations, our team will assist you with rebooking on the next available flight. We also help arrange accommodation if overnight stay is required. Additional costs due to airline issues are covered under our travel protection plan.

Yes, you can add extra services like airport transfers, spa treatments, excursions, and special meals through your dashboard or by contacting our support team. Additional services are subject to availability and may incur extra charges.
